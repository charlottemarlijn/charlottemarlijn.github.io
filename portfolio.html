Multiple worlds

The following videos are part of a 20 minute movie about a masked villain, made by my friends and I, creating visual effects using Adobe After Effects. The film features various references to pop culture, such as the show "Avatar: The Last Airbender", which is shown in the first video. The second video shows the masked villain caught on a security camera, travelling to a different "world" trough a portal. We employed different styles to show the different worlds that the masked villain visits. One of them was the LEGO world, and a short part of a LEGO stop motion clip created by me for the film is shown in the last video.
